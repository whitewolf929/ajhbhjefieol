Indian Institute of Information Technology, Ranchi




Welcome to the world of college, where we come with expectations but live with the realities.
So, here is the expectation vs reality of my college.

EXPECTATIONS

  • Large campus filled with greenery and appears to be in the lap of nature.
  • Big playground enough for all popular outdoor games and separate indoor gaming facility.
  • Atleast a canteen or cafe within campus(for anytime hunger).
  • Big hall for labs with any time accessibility.
  • A big library without timing restriction containing numerous books of almost every genre.
  • Focus less on acedemics and more on skill development.
  • Exciting cultural events and fest frequently.

REALITIES

  • Sharing, temporary campus along with restriction and lacks beauty of nature.
  • Very small playground insufficient for most of sporting activities and lacks in gaming facilities.
  • Neither canteen nor cafe fully dependent of mess food.
  • Conjusted lab rooms with only routine accessibility.
  • Very small library with very limited book.
  • High focus on acedemics(leading pressure), and very few skill oriented program.
  • Little or no culttural events.
